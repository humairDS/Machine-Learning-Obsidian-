# Biological Neuron 🧠

The idea of Artificial Neural Networks comes from how the human brain works.

A biological neuron consists of:

- **Dendrites** → Receive signals from other neurons
- **Cell Body (Soma)** → Processes information
- **Axon** → Sends output signal
- **Synapse** → Connection between neurons

### Example

Suppose you touch a hot cup.

1. Dendrites receive the signal.
2. Cell body processes it.
3. Axon sends the response.
4. Your hand quickly moves away.


```
Input Signals  
↓  
Dendrites  
↓  
Cell Body  
↓  
Axon  
↓  
Output Signal
```
